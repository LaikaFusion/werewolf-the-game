Welcome to Werewolf the Game!

Rules

Rules adapted from https://www.playwerewolf.co/rules/

Setting Up

Assemble a group of players. An odd number is best, although not absolutely mandatory. There should be at least seven players.

At the start of the game, each person will be assigned a role, either a Villager, a Seer (optional), a Doctor (optional), or a Werewolf.

Gameplay

The game proceeds in alternating night and day rounds. Begin with nighttime.

The Night

At night, the moderator tells all the players, "Close your eyes."

The moderator says, "Werewolves, open your eyes." The werewolves do so, and can identify each other in the Werewolves char. The moderator should also note who the werewolves are.
